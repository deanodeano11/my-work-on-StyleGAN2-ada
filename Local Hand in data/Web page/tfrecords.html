Converting your dataset to .tfrecords

After following the preparing your dataset instructions, conversion to .tfrecords is very easy. Note that the conversion process may take a few hours.

Simply follow these steps:

  1. Set your custom_dataset parameter to be the path of the folder created.
  2. Set your custom_images parameter to be the path of your formatted dataset.
  3. Run the cell to set those two variables
  4. Run the next cell to begin training

Once the conversion has completed utilise the cell titled "archive prepared datasets to google drive" to save your tf records to your google drive so they are not lost when the session is disconnected.
