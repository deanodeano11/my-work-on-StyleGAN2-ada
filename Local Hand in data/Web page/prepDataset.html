Making your images accessible on google colab

As of right now your images are stored on your google drive in a zipped folder, innaccessible by the local machine on colab, so follow the instructions on colab to do 3 things:

  1. Mount your Google drive to the colab virtual machine, so its files can be accessed
  2. Copy your zipped dataset from your google drive to the virtual machine
  3. Unzip the dataset once it is on the virtual machine

Running the code
